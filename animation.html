FIRE ALARM SIMULATOR

By Joshua Tomoyasu

The above SVG canvas simulates a Simplex fire alarm system. The system consists of two activation devices and one alert device. The first of two activation devices is a Simplex "T-Bar" pull station. To activate the system using the pull station, simply click on the the pull bar! The second activation device is a smoke detector. Hover your mouse over the detector to set off the system. To reset the system, click the lock on the pull station located directly above the handle with your imaginary Simplex CAT30 key! For the alert device, a Simplex TrueAlert ES horn-strobe combo is in use.

The above animation is inspired by real-life fire alarm components. The pull station is modeled after the one seen here. The a photo of a real Simplex TrueAlert ES can be seen here. The smoke detector isn't modeled after any smoke detector in particular. My inspiration for creating this piece is my interest in fire alarms. I based this simulation on a scratch project I was fond of which was very similar to this one.
